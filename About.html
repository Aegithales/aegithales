About me…

“Your mission is to solve the riddle of this world.”
(Count of the West in Three-body problem by Liu Cixin)

My name is Aegithales… well, not really. But my real name is not important.

I am just an average dude, holding a mundane daily job that has nothing to do with the ‘riddle of this world’. The riddle-solving is a hobby that seems rather introverted. No one is really interested in my riddles, nor in my solutions, not my kids, my wife, or my friends. So I publish them here. I hope you like them.

See you around, - Aegithales.
