Abiogenesis: no RNA world, but a single-step to LUCA.

  • Single-step abiogenesis means that the DNA, RNA, and proteins of LUCA were synthetized spontaneously on porous surfaces (of rocks and clay), through an extremely unlikely chain of events.

  • If there are infinitely many ELPs any low-probability event can occur somewhere, actually infinitely many times.
